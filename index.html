About me

My name is Steve, and I have been crafting furniture since I was old enough to hold a tool. I am one-third of a three generation wood working family. I learned from my dad, and taught my daughter how to design and build furniture as well. Some of my specialties include carving and handmade joints. From design to plans to hand-crafting furniture, we do it all here at Wood Generation.
